HIGHWATCH AI — OUR REFUND AND SATISFACTION POLICY
Version 1.2 | Simple & Transparent
================================================================

At Highwatch AI, we want you to love using our tools. If things aren't working out, we’re here to help make it right. Here is a plain-English guide to how our refunds work.

1. OUR 30-DAY PROMISE
----------------------
If you've signed up for a subscription and realize it's not the right fit for you within the first 30 days, we'll give you a full refund. No hard feelings!

2. HOW TO ASK FOR A REFUND
--------------------------
Just send a quick email to support@highwatch.ai. Tell us:
- Your account email.
- Why it wasn't working for you (this helps us get better!).
We usually process these in 3-5 business days.

3. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATIONS
-----------------------------
- Monthly Plans: You can cancel anytime. You’ll keep access until the end of your current month, and you won’t be charged again.
- Annual Plans: If you cancel an annual plan mid-year, we don't usually offer partial refunds, but you'll keep your "Pro" status until the year is up.

4. SPECIAL CASES
-----------------
- Technical Issues: If our system was down or had a major bug that stopped you from working, let us know. We’re happy to offer service credits or refunds regardless of the 30-day window.
- Custom Projects: For enterprise customers with custom contracts, the refund terms in your specific agreement will apply.

5. CHARGEBACKS
--------------
Before calling your bank, please talk to us! Most issues can be solved much faster with a simple conversation.

We’re here to help you succeed!
- The Highwatch Team
