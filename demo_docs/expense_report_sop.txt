HIGHWATCH AI — GETTING REIMBURSED: A SIMPLE GUIDE
How to File Your Expenses Without the Headache
================================================================

Let's be honest: nobody likes doing expense reports. But we want to make sure you’re not paying for company stuff out of your own pocket. Here’s the simplest way to get your money back.

1. WHAT CAN YOU CLAIM?
----------------------
Basically, anything you spend specifically for work. Common examples:
- Coffee or meals with clients.
- Travel (Uber, flights, hotels) for work trips.
- Software or tools you need for your job.
- Internet or phone bills (if you work remotely).

*Note:* If it's something big (like a $500 course), just get a quick "okay" from your manager first.

2. KEEP THOSE RECEIPTS!
-----------------------
The finance team needs receipts for everything over $10. A photo of the bill or a PDF of an online invoice works perfectly. If you lose a receipt, tell us—we can usually work something out.

3. HOW TO SUBMIT
----------------
1. Go to our Internal Portal.
2. Upload your receipt photos.
3. Add a quick note (e.g., "Lunch with potential client X").
4. Hit "Submit."

4. WHEN DO YOU GET PAID?
------------------------
We process expense reports twice a month (on the 15th and 30th). You’ll see the money show up in your bank account along with your next paycheck.

5. NEED HELP?
-------------
If you’re confused about a category or the portal is being glitchy, just ping #finance on Slack. They’re super helpful.

Thanks for keeping the wheels turning!
- Your Finance Team
