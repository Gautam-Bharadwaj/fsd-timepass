WELCOME TO HIGHWATCH AI! OUR TEAM HANDBOOK
Everything You Need to Know About Life Here
================================================================

Welcome aboard! We’re so excited to have you with us. Highwatch AI is more than just a company; we’re a group of people trying to build the future of document intelligence. This handbook is here to help you settle in and understand how we work.

1. OUR VALUES: WHAT WE STAND FOR
--------------------------------
- Trust First: We trust you to do your best work. We don't micromanage.
- Be Curious: Ask "why" and "how" every single day.
- Help Others: If a teammate is stuck, lend a hand.
- Ship It: Perfection shouldn't stop us from getting great things to our users.

2. WORK-LIFE BALANCE (REALLY!)
------------------------------
- Flexible Hours: As long as you’re making your meetings and getting your work done, we don’t care if you start at 8 AM or 11 AM.
- Remote Work: We love our office, but we love our homes too. Most of us work from home 2-3 days a week.
- Time Off: We offer 25 days of paid time off per year, plus sick leave. Please use them! We want you rested, not burned out.

3. BENEFITS & PERKS
-------------------
- Health Insurance: Comprehensive cover for you and your family.
- Learning Budget: We’ll pay for books, courses, or conferences that help you grow.
- Setup Allowance: Need a better chair or a second monitor? We've got a budget for that.

4. HOW WE COMMUNICATE
---------------------
- Slack: For quick chats and updates. (Pro tip: Use 'Do Not Disturb' when you need to focus).
- Zoom/Google Meet: For when a call is faster than a thread.
- Email: Mostly for external stuff.

5. FEEDBACK
-----------
We have a "flat" culture. If you have an idea to make the product better or the office more fun, tell someone! Whether it's your lead or the CEO, our doors (and Slack DMs) are always open.

We’re glad you’re here. Let’s build something amazing together!
